## Boundary

M4 owns Stage 1 closeout labels and public claim discipline. It does not own new
generation features, live provider adapters, or additional demo UI work.

## Final Claim Gate

Physical-execution claims require final embodiment records. Each record must
state the demo case, executor morphology, attempt date, pass/fail status, and
notes. Do not fabricate records or infer physical success from validation,
preview, critic score, or screenshots.
