## Boundary

M4 owns human reproducibility records and public claim discipline. It does not
own new generation features, live provider adapters, or additional demo UI work.

## Hard Blocker

At least five real human folding attempts are required. Each record must state
the demo case, who attempted it, attempt date, pass/fail status, and notes. Do
not fabricate records or infer human success from validation, preview, critic
score, or screenshots.
